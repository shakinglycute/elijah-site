Privacy policy for ElijahPaz.space

This site is a personal project run by Elijah Paz. It is not a business and is used for experimentation, learning, and vibes. Still, here is exactly what happens with your data.

What is collected

When you visit the site, the following information can be collected:

  • Page URL you visited and the tab you clicked.
  • The page you came from (referrer) if your browser sends it.
  • Basic browser/device info (user agent, platform, language, viewport size, screen size, color depth).
  • Technical details like CPU cores, approximate RAM, whether you appear online, and your Do Not Track setting.
  • Approximate location (country, region/state, city) and IP address from a geo‑lookup API.
  • Local timestamp (your browser’s date/time) of the visit.
  • Cookies only if you allow it: if you press “Allow cookie logging”, your browser’s document.cookie string may be logged for analytics until you clear site data or revoke consent.

How it is logged

  • Events are stored in your own browser using localStorage so Elijah can see local stats if needed.
  • The same event data is sent to a private Discord channel using a Discord webhook controlled only by Elijah.
  • No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising.

Cookies & consent

  • Cookie values are never logged unless you explicitly click “Allow cookie logging” in the banner.
  • If you allow, that choice is remembered in localStorage until you clear your browser data.
  • If you click “No thanks”, cookies are not logged for that visit and you will be asked again next time.

Your choices

  • You can block or clear cookies and localStorage in your browser settings at any time.
  • You can use a VPN or privacy tools to hide or change your IP and location.
  • If you don’t want any of this, simply don’t use the site.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or want something removed, contact Elijah at shakingly@gmail.com or through whatever channel you got this link from (Discord / friends / school).

This site can also log cookie values to Elijah’s private analytics log. This is just for nerd stats (no passwords or forms), and it’s optional.
